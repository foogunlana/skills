## Origin

> *Every meeting has a cost. Not just the time on the calendar, but the
> context switch before and after, the focus block it fragments, and
> the deep work that never happens. Calendar Audit makes that cost visible.*

## Key Concepts

- Every meeting is scored on 5 dimensions (Decision Power, Delegation, Async Potential, Frequency Fit, Duration Fit)
- Scores determine the zone: Green (keep), Yellow (optimise), Orange (restructure), Red (eliminate)
- Meeting memory tracks your decisions week-over-week
- The target is 60% focus / 40% meetings — not zero meetings
- Contiguous focus blocks matter more than total hours
